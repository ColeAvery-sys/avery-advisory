# Batch 19: Brand Empire Map, Asset Rights, and Cross-Promotion Engine

ATLAS Batch 19 connects the Media Baron system into one safer, clearer brand and offer map.

## Core Rule

ATLAS can map, draft, recommend, audit, and prepare.

ATLAS cannot publish, spend ad money, message customers, change prices, use assets with unknown rights, or make public claims without Cole approval.

## Modules

- `brandEmpireMapEngine.ts`: brand records, briefs, platform plans, offer maps, content pillars, and brand priority scoring.
- `offerLadderEngine.ts`: offers, ladders, missing offer detection, lead magnet ideas, upsell paths, and claim validation.
- `assetRightsTrackerEngine.ts`: asset rights records, commercial-use checks, proof links, public-use blocks, and public-use approval.
- `platformPolicyTrackerEngine.ts`: platform policy notes, risk checklists, workflow attachment, risky item flags, and review status.
- `crossPromotionEngine.ts`: cross-promo routes, CTA sets, best traffic routes, brand confusion risk, and calendar items.
- `productRepurposingEngine.ts`: one idea into YouTube, shorts, blog, newsletter, Etsy, merch, Amazon, Reddit, Instagram, Facebook, lead magnet, and service angles.
- `storefrontAuditEngine.ts`: storefront/page/listing audits for clarity, trust, SEO, visual, offer, CTA, proof, policy risk, and conversion risk.
- `adTestPlannerEngine.ts`: draft ad tests, ad copy, creative briefs, targeting ideas, budget recommendations, spend approval, manual launch, and results logging.
- `customerSupportDraftEngine.ts`: support message classification, draft replies, rewrites, sensitive escalation, and manual sent gating.
- `revenueAttributionEngine.ts`: revenue records, campaign links, source totals, attribution confidence, reports, best channel, and vanity metric detection.

## Safety Rules

1. Public-facing actions require Cole approval.
2. Rights-sensitive assets require review.
3. Unknown rights block public/commercial use.
4. Client assets cannot be reused without permission.
5. Trademark/copyright risks must be flagged.
6. Medical, legal, financial, funding, income, and public claims require review.
7. Platform policy uncertainty means manual review.
8. Ad spend requires approval.
9. Support replies are draft-only until approved.
10. Revenue attribution must separate confirmed from guessed.
11. Experimental brands should not outrank cash-flow brands without Cole approval.
