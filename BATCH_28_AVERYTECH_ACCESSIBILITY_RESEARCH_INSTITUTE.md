# Batch 28: AveryTech Accessibility Research Institute

Batch 28 turns AveryTech from a software startup into an accessibility innovation lab.

ATLAS can now manage research efforts, participants, pilot programs, accessibility testing, grant intelligence, outcome measurement, publication drafts, university partnerships, healthcare partnerships, and assistive technology roadmaps.

## Permanent Rule: Evidence Before Expansion

Accessibility products should move through:

```text
Research -> Prototype -> Pilot -> Outcome Measurement -> Grant Readiness -> Scale
```

before large investment.

ATLAS should actively discourage building accessibility products with no user testing or evidence gathering.

## Safety Rule

ATLAS can organize research, recruit participants, manage pilots, prepare grant materials, summarize findings, and recommend research priorities.

ATLAS cannot provide medical advice, store diagnosis records, make clinical or efficacy claims, approve studies, or submit grant applications without Cole approval and appropriate professional review.

## Backend Modules

- `researchInstituteSafety.ts`: shared research approval gates, risk flags, Evidence Before Expansion Rule, mission-fit scoring, and labels.
- `researchCommandEngine.ts`: central research dashboard for ATLAS Assist, EchoFrame, executive dysfunction tools, aphantasia research, autism support tools, smart glasses, and future accessibility products.
- `participantCrmEngine.ts`: participant records, interest areas, consent status, pilot matching, and summaries without medical records or diagnosis tracking.
- `pilotProgramManager.ts`: pilot program records, participant enrollment gates, check-ins, feedback prompts, and result summaries.
- `accessibilityTestingLab.ts`: usability tests, accessibility scores, and improvement recommendations.
- `grantIntelligenceEngine.ts`: grant records, probability scoring, priority scoring, ranking, and readiness packets.
- `outcomeMeasurementEngine.ts`: observed outcome records, summaries, and reports without medical efficacy claims.
- `researchPublicationBuilder.ts`: research summaries, white papers, case studies, executive summaries, and slide outlines.
- `universityPartnershipTracker.ts`: university contacts, meetings, research interests, and outreach briefs.
- `healthcarePartnershipTracker.ts`: clinics, hospitals, therapists, support organizations, pilot potential, and outreach briefs.
- `assistiveTechnologyRoadmapEngine.ts`: roadmaps for ATLAS Assist, EchoFrame, executive dysfunction tools, visual scheduling, smart glasses, and accessibility research platforms.
