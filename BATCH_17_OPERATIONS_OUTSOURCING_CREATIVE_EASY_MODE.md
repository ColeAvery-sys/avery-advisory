# Batch 17: Operations, Outsourcing, Creative Pipelines, and Easy Mode

ATLAS Batch 17 adds backend logic for practical operations, outsourced editing, contractor quality control, creative production pipelines, mission guardrails, and low-overwhelm Easy Mode decisions.

## Core Rule

ATLAS can organize, draft, score, recommend, prepare, route, and log.

ATLAS cannot auto-hire, auto-pay, auto-deliver, auto-publish, auto-print, auto-record, or contact people without Cole approval.

## Main Priorities

1. Creator Logistics and editing services for early cash.
2. ATLAS HQ as the company operating system.
3. ATLAS Assist and disability-aid AI for long-term funding.
4. Grants and funding pipeline.
5. Marketing and sales system.
6. Creative automation as reusable infrastructure.

Creative tools are useful only when they support cash, marketing, demos, product development, or reusable infrastructure.

## Modules

- `operationsCommandEngine.ts`: operations plans, mission tasks, distractions, delegation, and Easy Mode queues.
- `editorOutsourcingEngine.ts`: editor candidates, scoring, outreach drafts, test tasks, reviews, approval, and rejection.
- `editingFulfillmentEngine.ts`: Creator Logistics editing projects, task packets, editor assignment, profit margin, delivery packets, revisions, and manual delivery.
- `contractorQualityControlEngine.ts`: contractor submission reviews, QC checklists, scoring, revision requests, Cole review, contractor issues, and payment approval.
- `creativeAutomationEngine.ts`: creative projects, mission alignment, build plans, asset lists, Cursor/Codex prompts, and recommendations.
- `godotPipelineEngine.ts`: Godot 4 prototype projects, design briefs, scene checklists, prompts, export checklists, bugs, and prototype readiness.
- `blenderPipelineEngine.ts`: Blender assets, script prompts, asset/animation/export/printability checklists, and routing.
- `davinciPipelineEngine.ts`: DaVinci projects, Resolve prompts, shot lists, assets, export presets, QC checks, and fulfillment routing.
- `missionGuardrailEngine.ts`: mission alignment scoring, distraction risk, priority recommendation, and archive/schedule/do-now decisions.
- `easyModeDecisionEngine.ts`: one-decision cards, A/B/C/D selection, simple follow-ups, action creation, and decision logs.

## Safety Rules

1. Editor outreach requires approval.
2. Contractor approval requires Cole approval.
3. Editor assignment requires Cole approval.
4. Payment should be verified before paid editor work begins.
5. Contractor payment requires Cole approval.
6. Client delivery requires quality review and Cole approval.
7. DaVinci/client/public videos cannot publish or deliver automatically.
8. Godot builds cannot be auto-published.
9. Printable Blender assets require printability and safety review.
10. Creative projects with high distraction risk should be scheduled later or archived.
11. Easy Mode must reduce complexity without hiding risk.
12. Every Easy Mode decision must be logged.

## What This Unlocks

ATLAS can now protect the main mission while managing outsourced editors, editing fulfillment, contractor QC, Godot/Blender/DaVinci creative pipelines, and simple Easy Mode decisions that create logged actions and approval items where needed.
