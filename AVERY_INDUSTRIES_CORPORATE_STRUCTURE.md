# Avery Industries LLC Corporate Structure

Date updated: 2026-06-03

Founder, CEO, and Chief Systems Architect: Cole Avery, also known as Cole Ends.

Mission: build ethical technology, media, education, and community systems that improve people's lives.

This document is the corrected company-structure reference for Avery Industries LLC. It separates active priorities from future expansion so the company can look credible to investors, partners, contractors, and future staff.

## Executive Summary

Avery Industries LLC is structured as a founder-led, Ethical AI operating company with multiple future divisions. The current execution focus should remain narrow:

1. ATLAS HQ: company operating system and executive command layer.
2. Creator Logistics: near-term recurring revenue through creator services.
3. AveryTech: ethical AI, accessibility, and productivity technology.
4. Marketing / PR / Advertising: trust, visibility, and lead generation.

All other divisions are strategic expansion lanes until revenue, team capacity, and operational systems justify broader execution.

## Execution Rule

The company should not attempt to operate every division at once. The practical order is:

1. Build Creator Logistics revenue.
2. Build ATLAS HQ operating control.
3. Build AveryTech product and grant readiness.
4. Expand media and education.
5. Expand publishing, collectibles, entertainment, real estate, and ventures only after capacity exists.
