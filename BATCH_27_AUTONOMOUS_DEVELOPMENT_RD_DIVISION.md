# Batch 27: Autonomous Development and R&D Division

Batch 27 turns ATLAS into the CTO and R&D layer for Avery Industries LLC.

ATLAS can now prioritize inventions, software, games, assets, prototypes, accessibility research, smart-glasses ideas, 3D printing products, and future hardware.

## Permanent Rule: Prototype First

Any software project, game, hardware project, accessibility tool, or product idea must pass:

```text
Research -> Prototype -> Validation
```

before entering full development.

ATLAS should actively discourage large projects from skipping this process.

## Safety Rule

ATLAS can prioritize, organize, estimate, score, track, and recommend.

ATLAS cannot deploy production code, release products, order hardware, spend money, approve public research claims, or move a prototype into full development without Cole approval.

## Backend Modules

- `rdSafety.ts`: shared R&D priority tiers, approval gates, risk detection, Prototype First Rule, labels, and scoring.
- `rdCommandEngine.ts`: dashboard for inventions, apps, games, prototypes, experiments, and R&D routes.
- `softwareProjectManager.ts`: software project plans, effort estimates, dependencies, risks, owners, and statuses.
- `featureRoadmapEngine.ts`: feature creep prevention with mission, revenue, accessibility, complexity, risk, time, and maintenance scoring.
- `bugTrackerEngine.ts`: extended with R&D bug priority, category, and prioritized R&D bug views.
- `researchLabEngine.ts`: research notes, summaries, opportunities, grant angles, and product ideas.
- `experimentTrackerEngine.ts`: experiment stages from idea to validation, with approval gates.
- `godotStudioManager.ts`: Godot project management, game design docs, build plans, prototype validation, and scope creep blocks.
- `blenderAssetPipeline.ts`: Blender asset stages from concept to approved export.
- `printingPipeline.ts`: 3D printing concepts, printability checks, cost estimates, and prototype validation.
- `prototypeScoringEngine.ts`: final prototype score and Build / Prototype / Research / Archive recommendation.
