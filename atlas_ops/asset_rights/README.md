# Asset Rights Tracker

Track every generated or collected asset before public or commercial use.

Use `asset_rights_tracker.csv` for:

- Generated audio
- Generated images
- Video clips
- Design files
- Fonts
- Music and sound effects
- Client files
- Outsourced contractor assets
- Templates
- 3D models
- AI-generated assets

## Safety Rules

- Unknown rights block public and commercial use.
- Client assets are not reusable unless the client explicitly permits reuse.
- Copyrighted or trademarked characters, brands, music, fonts, and clips need review before use.
- AI-generated assets should be labeled and checked before publication.
- Public or commercial use requires Cole approval when rights are uncertain.
