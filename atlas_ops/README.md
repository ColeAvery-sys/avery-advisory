# ATLAS Ops

This folder is the plain-English operating layer for ATLAS. It uses Markdown and CSV first so Cole can inspect, edit, and understand everything without a database.

## Folders

- `intake/`: raw ideas, requests, leads, and future intake forms before ATLAS routes them.
- `audio_ops/`: ElevenLabs and voice/audio generation policies, request forms, and usage logs.
- `asset_rights/`: rights records for generated audio, images, video, fonts, client files, outsourced files, and public-use assets.
- `approval_gates/`: policies and logs for anything that needs Cole approval.
- `logs/`: simple CSV logs for intake, actions, automation runs, and operational history.
- `reports/`: reusable reports, including daily CEO report templates.
- `prompts/`: copy/paste prompts for Codex, Cursor, ATLAS, ElevenLabs, sales, scripts, and rights checks.
- `exports/`: safe local exports, summaries, CSVs, and handoff files.
- `templates/`: reusable operator templates and forms.
- `creator_logistics/`: Creator Logistics service menu, client intake, and fulfillment docs.
- `career_os/`: Career profile source-of-truth notes, LinkedIn sync outputs, and job-application drafts.
- `new_prometheus/`: The New Prometheus content system, pillars, and video ideas.
- `averytech/`: AveryTech specs, Easy Mode notes, product/company docs, and public-facing app plans.
- `media_baron/`: platform matrix, reuse maps, sales-channel planning, and media empire operations.

## Operating Rule

ATLAS can organize, draft, recommend, and prepare. ATLAS does not publish, send, spend, upload, hire, pay, or change live accounts without Cole approval.
