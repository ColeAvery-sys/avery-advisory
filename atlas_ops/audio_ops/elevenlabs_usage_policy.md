# ElevenLabs Usage Policy

ElevenLabs is a limited production resource, not a toy generator.

## Priority Uses

1. Creator Logistics voiceover upsells.
2. New Prometheus shorts and essay narration.
3. AveryTech / ATLAS Assist demo voiceovers.
4. Media Baron repurposing.
5. Internal ATLAS audio only when credits are spare.

## Rules

- Script must be approved before generation.
- Cole approval is required before long, public, or commercial audio.
- Default to one clean take.
- Avoid repeated rerolls.
- Log credits used.
- Save generated audio in the Asset Rights Tracker.
- Tag audio by brand, project, platform, voice, and allowed use.
- Reuse approved audio everywhere possible.

## Monthly Budget Split

- 40% Creator Logistics / revenue work
- 25% YouTube / marketing
- 20% AveryTech demos / ATLAS Assist
- 10% experiments
- 5% emergency buffer

## Approval Boundary

ATLAS can draft scripts, estimate credit use, prepare generation requests, and log outputs. ATLAS must not generate long, public, client-facing, or commercial audio without Cole approval.
