# Creator Logistics

Creator Logistics is the content infrastructure division for creators and small businesses.

It helps turn scattered footage, ideas, clips, timestamps, voiceovers, uploads, and content plans into a repeatable system. The goal is early cash flow through practical service delivery while keeping client promises scoped, reviewed, and approved.

## Operating Rules

- Client-facing messages require Cole approval.
- Final pricing requires Cole approval.
- Delivery packets require Cole approval.
- Voiceover generation follows the ElevenLabs usage policy.
- Client assets stay private unless reuse is explicitly approved.
