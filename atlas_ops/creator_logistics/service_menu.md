# Creator Logistics Service Menu

## 1. Short-Form Clip Package

Turn existing long-form content into short-form clip opportunities, hooks, labels, captions notes, and posting ideas.

## 2. Long-Form Editing Package

Organize and edit long-form videos into a cleaner upload-ready draft package.

## 3. Voiceover Add-On

Prepare approved voiceover scripts and generate approved audio when credits and rights allow.

## 4. Upload / SEO Package

Prepare YouTube titles, descriptions, tags, chapters, thumbnail briefs, pinned comments, and upload notes.

## 5. Monthly Content Infrastructure Support

Recurring support for content organization, calendars, clip plans, uploads, repurposing, and campaign structure.

## 6. Creator Rescue Package

A focused cleanup package for creators with scattered footage, missed deadlines, unfinished videos, or a content backlog.
