# Batch 23: Audience, Community, And Fanbase Growth System

Batch 23 builds audience infrastructure so ATLAS can understand who watched, cared, returned, bought, supported, shared, became a lead, or became a superfan.

## Modules

- `audienceSafety.ts`: shared helpers for audience risk detection, sentiment, engagement scoring, draft-only outreach, and category counting.
- `audienceCommandEngine.ts`: audience snapshots, dashboard summaries, top segments, community risks, and audience recommendations.
- `communityCrmEngine.ts`: community contacts, relationship updates, relationship views, follow-up drafts, thank-you drafts, and collaboration drafts.
- `commentIntelligenceEngine.ts`: comment records, categorization, comment analysis, common questions, leads, partners, risks, and content ideas.
- `fanSupporterEngine.ts`: supporter records, supporter scores, supporter levels, superfan identification, and respectful action drafts.
- `newsletterGrowthEngine.ts`: newsletter records, newsletter health, idea generation, subject lines, welcome sequences, and lead magnet ideas.
- `communityHubPlanner.ts`: future community hub plans, channels, rules, moderation plans, and event ideas.
- `audienceResearchEngine.ts`: research datasets, top topics, growing interests, content/product/service/lead magnet ideas, and grant evidence.
- `feedbackMiningEngine.ts`: feedback records, classification, mined feature/product/community/pain-point reports, and opportunity reports.
- `pollSurveyEngine.ts`: surveys, polls, result analysis, and insight generation.
- `superfanPipelineEngine.ts`: superfan records, stage recommendations, approved stage advancement, action drafts, and advocate identification.

## Safety Rules

- No impersonation.
- No automatic messages.
- No mass-DMs.
- No community manipulation.
- No fake engagement.
- Sensitive refund, legal, medical, financial, harassment, and safety issues escalate for review.
- Drafts are suggestions only and require approval before outreach.
