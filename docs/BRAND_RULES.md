# Brand Rules

## Company Name
- Use `Avery Industries LLC` everywhere.
- Never use `Avery Industries LLC`.

## ATLAS Positioning
- `ATLAS HQ` is the internal operating system.
- It should feel precise, executive, and reliable.
- It should never feel gimmicky, playful, or toy-like in production surfaces.

## Visual Style
- Dense, readable, dashboard-first layout
- clean hierarchy
- simple navigation
- mobile-friendly behavior
- minimal decorative noise
- table and panel layouts over oversized marketing blocks

## Voice
- direct
- operational
- concise
- professional
- approval-aware

## Messaging Rules
- Focus on utility, automation, and control.
- Emphasize revenue, operations, grants, content, and approvals.
- Do not imply automatic external action without approval.
