# Real Estate Wholesaling

Date created: 2026-06-06

Purpose: give Avery Industries LLC a controlled real-estate entry point that starts with wholesaling before rentals, commercial holdings, or development.

## Operating Model

1. Capture the lead.
2. Underwrite the numbers.
3. Flag the approval gate.
4. Draft the offer only.
5. Market to buyers only after approval.
6. Track the assignment, title, and closing path.
7. Log the result in the company memory layer.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- No offer goes out without Cole approval.
- No signature goes out without Cole approval.
- No buyer-facing outreach goes out without Cole approval.
- No deposits, hard money commitments, or closing instructions go out without Cole approval.
- Every lead must have a written exit plan and due diligence checklist.

## Phase 1 Scope

- Source distressed or motivated seller leads.
- Estimate ARV, repairs, spread, and assignment fee ceiling.
- Rank opportunities by deal quality and approval risk.
- Draft offers and due diligence packets locally.
- Keep every action auditable.

## Validation Checklist

- lead is captured
- numbers are underwritten
- approval gate is visible
- offer stays draft-only
- due diligence checklist exists
- ranking output is deterministic
- no external send occurs before approval
