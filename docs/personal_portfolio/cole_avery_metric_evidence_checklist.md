# Cole Avery Metric Evidence Checklist

Date created: 2026-06-03

Purpose: turn strong reported claims into investor/client-ready verified claims.

## Social Reach Claim

Current portfolio language:

`Reported 50M+ cross-platform views across X/Twitter, Facebook, Reddit, YouTube, and Twitch campaigns.`

Proof to attach:

- YouTube Analytics screenshots or exports.
- Facebook/Page/Business Suite analytics screenshots or exports.
- X/Twitter analytics screenshots or exports.
- Reddit post/community analytics screenshots.
- Twitch dashboard screenshots or campaign reports.
- Campaign date ranges.
- Campaign names.
- Links to public posts/videos where possible.
- Summary table showing platform, campaign, views, date range, and source.

## Creator Logistics Quality Claim

Current portfolio language:

`Reported 100% 5-star quality rating for editing-service delivery.`

Proof to attach:

- Client review screenshots.
- Marketplace profile rating screenshots.
- Email/testimonial permission records.
- Project delivery examples.
- Before/after editing examples.
- Number of reviewed projects.
- Review date range.

## Investor-Safe Rule

Until proof is attached, keep the word `reported` in front of these metrics.

Once proof is attached and stored, update the portfolio language from:

`Reported 50M+ views`

to:

`Documented 50M+ views across verified campaign reports`

Only use verified language when the evidence is actually available.
