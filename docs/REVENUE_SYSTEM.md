# Revenue System

## Goal
Build revenue systems that help `Avery Industries LLC` generate, track, approve, and deliver work without losing control of quality or permissions.

## Core Revenue Tracks
1. Creator services
2. Content operations
3. Grant support workflows
4. Productized internal tools
5. Publishing and IP packaging
6. Future software and division services

## Required Workflow
1. Capture the lead or opportunity.
2. Score fit, value, and risk.
3. Route through approval gates.
4. Generate drafts or scope packets.
5. Log the action and the owner.
6. Only send externally after user approval.

## System Rules
- No automatic outbound send.
- No unsupervised client deliverables.
- No hidden pricing or silent commitments.
- Every revenue action must be traceable.
- Every handoff must record status and next step.

## Validation Checklist
- lead is captured
- opportunity is scored
- approval gate is visible
- draft output is correct
- audit log is written
- no external send occurs before approval
