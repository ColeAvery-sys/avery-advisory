## Usage Instructions

### Discovery Call Playbook

Use this during consultation calls to guide the conversation, qualify the lead, and close the next step cleanly.

### Automation Audit Template

Use this to produce a structured review of the client's current systems, bottlenecks, and improvement opportunities.

### Proposal Template

Use this when a lead is ready for a formal scope, investment summary, and terms.

### Client Onboarding Checklist

Use this after payment is received to keep the handoff organized and reduce missed steps.

### Follow-Up Sequence

Use this as the basis for manual or semi-automated email follow-up after calls, proposals, or project completion.

### FAQ

Use this as a reference for website copy, email replies, and outreach responses.

## Suggested Sales Workflow

1. Lead submits a form
2. Cole reviews the lead and selects the right offer
3. Discovery call is held using the playbook
4. If fit is confirmed, send the proposal
5. Client pays through Stripe
6. Onboarding checklist starts
7. Work begins after access and files are received
8. Follow-up sequence handles reminders, proposal nudges, and project completion
