## Best Way To Send

1. Do not mass-send the full packet to hundreds of people.
2. Build a focused target list of 25-50 strategic recipients.
3. Prioritize warm introductions first.
4. Send one concise email with one secure link, not several large attachments.
5. Track every recipient in a sheet or CRM with sent date, follow-up date, status, notes, and next action.
6. Follow up after 5-7 business days.
7. Use "investment and strategic partnership review" language, not desperate fundraising language.
8. Ask for a 20-minute call.
9. Keep unverified claims labeled as "reported" until proof is attached.
10. Do not sign or verbally agree to investment terms without attorney and tax review.
