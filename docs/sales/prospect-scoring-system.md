# Prospect Scoring System

## Purpose

Score prospects from 1-10 so outreach focuses on the best opportunities first.

## Score Criteria

### Upload Consistency

- 1: Inactive or sporadic
- 5: Some consistency
- 10: Clear, regular publishing

### Audience Size

- 1: Very small or unclear audience
- 5: Moderate audience
- 10: Strong audience with clear reach

### Editing Quality

- 1: Rough or distracting edits
- 5: Mixed quality
- 10: Clean, polished, professional

### Thumbnail Quality

- 1: Weak packaging
- 5: Average packaging
- 10: Strong, clickable packaging

### Monetization Potential

- 1: Little obvious buyer intent
- 5: Some commercial fit
- 10: Clear path to paid support

### Responsiveness

- 1: Hard to contact or inactive
- 5: Some response potential
- 10: Easy to reach and likely to reply

## Scoring Method

Add the six criteria together and divide by 6 for the final score.

### Interpretation

- 1-3: Low priority
- 4-6: Medium priority
- 7-8: Strong prospect
- 9-10: Top priority

## Outreach Priority

Prioritize prospects with:

- Score above 7
- Recent uploads or visible activity
- Obvious content packaging issues
- A clear business or monetization angle
