# Outreach Sequence

## DM Sequence

### DM 1: Initial Reach-Out

Hey [Name], I took a quick look at your channel and noticed a few opportunities to improve views, retention, and content structure. I’m offering free creator audits through Avery Advisory and would be happy to review your channel if you want an outside eye.

### DM 2: Follow-Up

Just circling back in case this got buried. If you want, send me your channel and I’ll take a quick look and share a few practical opportunities you can use right away.

### DM 3: Soft Close

Last note from me. If now is not the right time, no problem at all. If you do want a free channel audit later, just reply with your channel link and I’ll take a look.

### DM 4: After They Reply

Perfect. Send me the channel link and I’ll review it. I’ll keep it focused on views, retention, content structure, and the easiest wins.

## Audit Delivery Flow

1. Prospect replies with a channel link
2. Review the channel quickly for the biggest opportunities
3. Send a short summary of findings
4. Invite them to a discovery call if there is a clear fit
5. If the call goes well, move to proposal and payment
