# Prospect Research Guide

## Objective

Build a target list for Avery Advisory and Creator Logistics with a simple research process that identifies who is likely to say yes to a free audit or a creator service offer.

## Ideal Creator

- 1k-100k subscribers
- Uploading consistently
- Weak thumbnails
- Weak shorts strategy
- Poor SEO
- No team

## Ideal Local Business

- Poor social media
- No automation
- Weak website
- Service business
- Established revenue

## What To Look For

### YouTube

- Gaming channels with inconsistent packaging
- Commentary channels with weak thumbnails
- Education channels with clear expertise but poor video structure
- Podcast clips channels that are under-edited or poorly repurposed

### Twitch

- Active streamers
- Little or no YouTube presence
- No clipping strategy
- Weak post-stream repurposing

### Local Business

- Contractors
- HVAC
- Plumbers
- Lawyers
- Real estate agents
- Auto shops

## Research Steps

1. Find the channel or business
2. Confirm they fit the target profile
3. Note the last upload or recent activity
4. Identify the biggest visible bottleneck
5. Decide whether the best entry is a free audit, consultation, or direct offer
6. Add them to the pipeline CSV

## Contact Priority

- Email when publicly available and relevant
- DM when the platform is the main audience touchpoint
- Website form when no better method exists

## Notes To Capture

- First impression
- Channel or website issues
- Recent activity
- Possible package fit
- Best message angle
