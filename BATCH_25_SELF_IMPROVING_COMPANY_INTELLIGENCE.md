# Batch 25: Self-Improving Company Intelligence Layer

Batch 25 gives ATLAS a local company brain.

ATLAS can now store company memory, turn outcomes into lessons, analyze wins and losses, discover opportunities, detect patterns, map business relationships, draft SOPs, estimate outcomes, recommend next moves, and preserve institutional knowledge.

## Safety Rule

ATLAS can analyze, remember, score, recommend, and summarize.

ATLAS cannot automatically change company strategy, pricing, hiring, publishing, finances, policies, client commitments, or public claims. Recommendations remain advisory until Cole approves.

## Backend Modules

- `companyIntelligenceSafety.ts`: shared approval gates, strategic risk flags, confidence scoring, labels, and counts.
- `companyMemoryEngine.ts`: memory records for projects, clients, products, videos, contractors, meetings, decisions, failures, and successes.
- `lessonsLearnedEngine.ts`: lessons from decisions, outcomes, and company events, with SOP/project attachment and future-project application.
- `winLossAnalyzer.ts`: win/loss classification, success patterns, failure patterns, and recommendations.
- `opportunityDiscoveryEngine.ts`: repeated demand detection for revenue, grant, content, automation, and hiring opportunities.
- `patternDetectionEngine.ts`: recurring revenue, content, client complaint, and grant patterns.
- `knowledgeGraphEngine.ts`: local business relationship graph for clients, products, revenue, channels, audience, content, and sales.
- `sopBuilderEngine.ts`: SOP drafts from repeated successful projects, checklists, lessons, and Cole approval.
- `predictionEngine.ts`: estimate-only client, product, grant, video, and deadline predictions.
- `recommendationEngine.ts`: central advisory outputs for do more, do less, stop, automate, delegate, and build next.
- `institutionalMemoryVault.ts`: durable vault for SOPs, policies, brand guides, lessons, decisions, templates, systems, and continuity packets.

## What It Unlocks

ATLAS can now say:

- The last repeated client requests suggest a thumbnail service add-on.
- YouTube Shorts are repeatedly linked to Creator Logistics leads.
- This SOP should be drafted from successful editing projects.
- This prediction is an estimate, not a guarantee.
- This recommendation changes strategy, so Cole approval is required.
- If everyone vanished tomorrow, the Avery Industries LLC Brain starts with the institutional memory vault.
