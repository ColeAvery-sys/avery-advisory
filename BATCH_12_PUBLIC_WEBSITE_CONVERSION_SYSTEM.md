# ATLAS Batch 12: Public Website, Landing Pages, and Conversion System

Batch 12 turns AveryTech and ATLAS into a safer public-facing lead and credibility system.

Public pages can explain AveryTech, ATLAS Assist, Creator Logistics, partnerships, funding credibility, case studies, demos, pricing ranges, lead magnets, and analytics.

Public pages cannot promise medical outcomes, guarantee creator growth, guarantee funding, imply nonprofit status without verification, or publish proof without permission and Cole approval.

## Backend Engines

- `websiteLeadRouter.ts`: classifies and routes website leads into CRM, agent inbox, and Action Center draft replies.
- `landingPageContentEngine.ts`: generates public-safe landing page copy and validates claims.
- `atlasAssistInterestEngine.ts`: handles ATLAS Assist interest and flags sensitive medical details.
- `creatorLogisticsSalesEngine.ts`: recommends packages, estimates safe price ranges, and drafts proposals.
- `funderCredibilityEngine.ts`: generates funder-safe summaries and validates funding claims.
- `partnerPageIntakeEngine.ts`: handles partner inquiries and reply drafts.
- `caseStudyProofEngine.ts`: gates public proof by permission and Cole approval.
- `demoRequestEngine.ts`: routes demo requests and creates prep checklists.
- `leadMagnetEngine.ts`: captures consented lead magnet requests and drafts follow-ups.
- `websiteAnalyticsEngine.ts`: privacy-conscious event tracking and conversion summaries.

## Safety Rules

1. Public pages can explain AveryTech, ATLAS, Creator Logistics, and product concepts.
2. Public pages can collect leads, demo requests, partner inquiries, and interest forms.
3. Public pages cannot promise medical outcomes.
4. Public pages cannot guarantee creator growth.
5. Public pages cannot guarantee funding.
6. Public pages cannot imply nonprofit status unless verified.
7. Public pages cannot claim partnerships, testimonials, or results without permission and evidence.
8. ATLAS cannot automatically accept clients from website forms.
9. ATLAS cannot automatically send replies to website leads.
10. ATLAS cannot publish testimonials or case studies without permission and Cole approval.
11. ATLAS cannot collect unnecessary sensitive medical details.
12. Every external reply goes to Action Center.
13. Every lead should be routed to the correct ATLAS agent.
14. Website analytics should be privacy-conscious.
