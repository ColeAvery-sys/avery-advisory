## Executive Summary

Avery Industries LLC is being designed as an AI-assisted holding company.

The long-term goal is to create a centralized operating system, ATLAS, capable of coordinating multiple business divisions, creative projects, products, charitable initiatives, and revenue streams through human leadership and AI management.

This document establishes:

- Organizational structure
- Department responsibilities
- Agent hierarchy
- Approval authority
- Engineering priorities
- Development standards

This document serves as the source of truth for future development.
